NOTICE TO CUSTOMERS IN THE EUROPEAN UNION:

Use of FM transmitters is currently illegal in certain European countries due to specific radio frequency restrictions. You must check the local regulations in all European countries in which you intend to use the FM transmitter to ensure its legality. You accept full responsibility for complying with all such laws and regulations applicable in your particular country of operation.

Usage Prohibited

(as of December 29, 2006)

The following countries allow low power FM transmitters that are in compliance with EN 301 357-2 to be sold but not used:

  • France
  • Greece
  • Italy
  • The Netherlands
  • Slovenia

License Required

(as of December 29, 2006)

The following countries allow low power FM transmitters that are in compliance with EN 301 357-2 to be sold and used, but the user must obtain a license prior to using the device:

  • Hungary
  • Poland
