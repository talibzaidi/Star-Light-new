BlueChart® g2 Vision®/BlueChart g2

BlueChart g2 Vision and BlueChart g2 give you detailed offshore marine maps to help you navigate. Realistic navigation features show everything from shaded depth contours and coastlines to spot soundings, navaids, port plans, wrecks, obstructions, intertidal zones, restricted areas and IALA symbols.

BlueChart g2 Vision

BlueChart g2 Vision offers the same rich detailed charting of BlueChart g2, and adds Garmin Auto Guidance technology, a growing database of high-resolution satellite imagery, above and below the water 3-D perspective, aerial photographs for real world reference and coastal roads with points of interest.

BlueChart g2

BlueChart g2 is a more economical option for our value-end chartplotters. It has safety depth settings to let you select up to 5 values for specific depth contour intervals. And the fishing charts emphasize bathymetric contours with less visual clutter on the display.

There's also a choice of standard 2-D direct overhead or 3-D “over the bow” map perspective views to make chart reading and orientation easier.
