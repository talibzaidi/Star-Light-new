Garmin's Bluetooth Smart Products Compatibility

Bluetooth Smart is a low energy, power-efficient technology that can be found in some of the newer Smartphones on the market today.

Several Garmin devices utilize Bluetooth Smart to wirelessly sync with these Smartphones when they are running the Garmin Connect Mobile app.

The following phones have been tested by Garmin and deemed compatible to work with our Bluetooth Smart devices:

  • Android
  • Apple
  • Windows 10 Phone

Is your phone not listed?

Garmin will attempt to keep this list up to date with the most popular Smartphones that feature Bluetooth Smart technology however it will be possible that a particular model may exist and appear to work but not be listed as compatible.
