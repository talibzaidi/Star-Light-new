What kind of data card does your Garmin unit take?

Garmin offers detailed marine maps and charts on both Garmin preprogrammed data cards and microSD cards, making it easy and convenient to load detailed charts to your compatible Garmin. These data cards do not include any software for use on a PC. See the product compatibility lists below to determine which card your product takes.
