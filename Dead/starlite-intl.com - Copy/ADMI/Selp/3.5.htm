3.5 Administrative Functions

This is a very important page for your site. This page follows rules which are identical to the rules in the Product Pages. However the information you enter here is used to personalize your site and is used on both the homepage and the contact page.

If you do not enter text into either text box you will not have text on your hompage (unless there is pre-existing text).

This page will also change the username and password for the sanction tool.

Great care should be taken if you modify your username and password, there are very few ways to re-set them and none of those are pleasant. So before you submit the page, make sure your username and password match what you wish them to be.

SECURITY ALERT:

Picking easy to guess usernames and passwords is a very bad idea and could lead to the loss of information in your site. We recommend you back-up your site as often as is feasible using a 4 tape ( disk etc. ) system, to protect your site.

BAD USERNAME AND PASSWORD COMBINATION: starlite / litestar

GOOD USERNAME AND PASSWORD: 6star94lite3cvz / 34lu9fuzzy55

As you can see it is easy to guess one of these pairs and not the other. Be pro-active in your safety and choose good passwords. Also do not write down a password unless you have to, and store it only in secure areas.
