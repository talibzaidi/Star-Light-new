2.4 Uploading of Images

The uploading of images is a simple task. Simply use the 'Browse' button on the page to select and appropriately named gif off of your computer or network and click on submit to send the image on its way. Remeber section 2.2 for naming your Images. Images that are not named in the product will not display.

Note that each product uses 2 images. One small image or thumbnail [ 100*x pix. ] and one larger image [ 0>325*x pix. ]. The only way to delete images is to find unused images in your ftp client under the Imi directory and manually delete them. For this reason we suggest you name your images using the following naming convention;

THUMBNAIL: productidt.gif > 64286t.gif

IMAGE: productid.gif > 64286.gif

( or jpg / jpeg / etc. )

In this way you can easily track down images belonging to items you have deleted. We suggest that when you delete a product you write it down for future reference. Finding unused images can be a trying task later on and web-space costs money, so you don't want too many unused pictures in there.
