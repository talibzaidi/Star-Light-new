2.1.1 Adding A Client

  The Add a Client Page is both Straightforward and daunting at the same time. Once you have used Sanction a few times you will have learned the benefits and limitations of the system. Sanction was designed to make updating the site easier for all involved but does require some training to use.

All fields accept up to 200 characters except for Description which takes 255 and memo fields which can take up to 65536 characters. However, just because a field accepts a certain length of information, does not mean it has to be filled up completely. The more information in the database the slower it will eventually get.

Currently no fields accept the ' character.

You must fill out a company name and description as the bare minimum to submit your client.

Click this button when you are ready to submit the form and thus enter the client into the database.

City : Chances are this option has been filled out for you. Otherwise choose a city from the drop down box. Only the higest levels of Access allow you to choose any city.
Company Name: This is the company name field and will be the name that users see when they use Ontario Ciries.
Proposed URL: If the company has it's own webstite already, replace the information in the field with the address of their site in the form of:

http://their-address-here

ELSE...

../client/CLIENTLVL.asp?CoName=CO-NAME

The above information is the URL ( address ) to use to create a Level Page for a client. Replace CLIENTLVL with a Level Page name, to see all our level pages, use the drop down box below. Otherwise type in the name of the level you would like, i.e. LevelTwoM.asp. Next replace the CO-NAME with the name of the Company. You can accomplish this easily by selecting the text in the Company Name field with your mouse, and then hitting CTRL + C to copy the company name. Next you select the word CO-NAME with your mouse. While that word, and only that word, is selected. Press CTRL + V to paste the Company Name into the field. You must replace any Spaces with %20 and cannot use apostrophe's ( ' ) at all. A future release may address these issues. You will have problems if you do not watch for spaces, delete any periods, or change any other part of the URL.
Choose from:  CLIENTONE     CLIENTTWO     CLIENTTHRE

COMPANYNAMElogo.gif
