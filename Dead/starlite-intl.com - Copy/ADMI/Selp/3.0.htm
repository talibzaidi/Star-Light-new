3.0 Logging Out

  It is important to logout using the Logout Link found at the bottom of the HomePage when you are done.

  If you choose not to log out it is possible that someone with malicious intent could find a way to access your part of the system. If this happens you could well imagine the chaos that would ensue. At the very least you will lose hours of work , and time is money.
