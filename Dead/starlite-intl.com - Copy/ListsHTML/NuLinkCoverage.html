Garmin nüLink! services are available in select coverage areas in the U.S. and Canada.

U.S. nüLink! Coverage

Coverage for the U.S. nüvi 1690 and nüLink! 1695 is provided by AT&T and includes extensive coverage of metro areas. You will need to enter an address or zip code to check the availability of service in your area. 

Canada nüLink! Coverage

Coverage for the Canadian nüvi 1690 and nüLink! 1695 is provided by Rogers and includes country-wide coverage. An optional roaming package for U.S. coverage is available.

¹Does not include coverage areas for traffic information. nüLink! traffic services are available in select areas.
