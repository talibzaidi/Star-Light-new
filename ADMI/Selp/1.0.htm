1.0 Logging In

  If you are reading this page chances are you have already logged into the Sanction system, or someone has logged in for you. The Sanction Log-In page can be found at the address that was given to you for your access to Sanction. The system has been designed to provide a reasonable amount of security to both the Host and the Clients. The login page is very straigthforward and has only two input boxes. The first box is for your Username, the second for your Password. When you have entered both fields, click on the grey submit button to log into the system. You will notice that the information you enter into the fields appears as |*'s| this is by design and is not an error.

  If after hitting the submit button you return back to the login page with the word 'Invalid' below the submit button, you have entered your information incorrectly. And must try again. Re-enter your Username and Password and try again. If you fail repeatedly contact your system administrator for help. Otherwise you should see a page that looks like this.
