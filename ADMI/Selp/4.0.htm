4.0 Programming Notes

* EQUATIONS: The follwing equations are used in this site and are hard coded ( cannot be altered via sanction ). Changing a rate or value in sanction may affect prices and values dramatically.

* BACKUP, BACKUP, BACKUP: I cannot stress how important this is. If for any reason any files become damaged or are altered by malicious individuals you will need a recent working backup to restore to, or you will run the risk of losing data. Once the site is installed and functioning on your ISP it is your responsibility to ensure that it remains so. I reccomend a rotating four week / four tape backup system. In this way you will stand to lose only one week of data ( more if you ignore the problem ). Daily incremental backups are never a bad idea either.

* PAGE DISPLAYS AN ERROR MESSAGE OF SOME SORT: It is likely that you have entered invalid information into the sanction tool and must go back a page or two to correct your input. It is not possible to anticipate all possible user input, yet the site should and will perform under normal conditions.

If the error occurs on one page but not others ( for one detail page but not another ), 99.9% this will be because invalid or damaging code has been entered via the sanction tool. Try editing or deleting the offending Item to resolve this issue.

The site worked yesterday but not at all today. This problem is most often caused by changes on your ISP server. Sometimes the good people at your ISP make changes and forget about special conditions that may apply to your site.
