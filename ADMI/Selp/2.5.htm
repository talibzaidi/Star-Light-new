2.5 Editing Banners

When choosing the 'Edit Banner' link from the homepage you are sent to a page with three options.

1) Edit an Existing Banner
2) Add a Banner
3) LIst Banners

1) Editing a banner is simple. Simply choose a banner from the drop down box and click on 'Edit"to continue. Which brings you to the following page.

Simply edit the fields which need to be changed and click the 'Add' button. Make sure the city and area are correctly selected. The most notable part of this page is the 'Ad Visible' checkbox. If you uncheck this box the banner will no longer be shown but will remain in the databse.

2) Adding Banners

The Banner Add page is essentialy the same as the banner edit page, however, all the fields are blank, except for the image URL. Fillout the form by entering the Company Name into the Ad Name field. Then pick an Area. If you want the ad to be seen leave the Ad Visible box checked, otherwise delselect it ( in caes where payment is not yet recieved etc. ). Next enter in the URL the banner points to such as: (Yahoo!)

http://www.yahoo.com

If you want the banner to point to a Certain Page, then follow these steps. 1. Go to the area in your client. 2. Find a link in the site ( what you click to get to where you want to go ). 3. Right-click on the link to their level and select open in new window. This should open a new browser window showing the desired page.

When you have opened the link in a new page use your mouse to select the Adress/URL

http://www.ontariocities.com/client/clientone.asp?CoName=IAC

with your mouse and use CTRL + C to copy it. Go back to the browser with your banner ad and put your mouse cursor into the Banner URL box (click in the box) and hit CTRL + V to paste the address. Thats it.

The last step is to enter in the URL of the Image [ 350*60 pix. ] for the banner. If you have already uploaded an image with the Image Upload feature (2.4) or are going to, simply replace the three (3) stars (*) in the image URL with the name of your image (without the extension). Example:

I upload : JimBobsHuntingBan1.gif ( which is the company name without spaces, and Ban for Banner, the 1 is because Jim Bob has paid for three banners and this is the first.

I enter into the Image url box the part in white.

http://www.ontariocities.com/clientimages/JimBobsHuntingBan1.gif

That's it. It's that easy.
