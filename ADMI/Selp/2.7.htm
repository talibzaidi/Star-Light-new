2.7 Changing Rates

The rates page is very straightforward. Modify the rates in decimal format. Each rate is a [ double ] number and is used as a multiplier in all calculations and can be either positive or negative. To set a tax rate of 50% Just put 0.5 into either tax box. The calculations used in this site can be seen under the programming notes.
