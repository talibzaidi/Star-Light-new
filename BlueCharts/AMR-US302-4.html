Region US302-South East Coast-Bathymetric, JAN 04 Detailed bathymetric coverage of offshore canyons from Hatteras Canyon through Blake Canyon including Blake Escarpment, Richardson Hills, and Harrington Hill. Also includes general coverage of the East Coast from Southport, NC to Naples, FL including Grand Bahama Island, Abaco Island, Eleuthera Island, and the Florida Keys. This is a fishing chart and should be used as a supplement to detailed BlueChart cartography. AMR-US302-4
