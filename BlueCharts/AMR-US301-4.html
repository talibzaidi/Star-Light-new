Region US301-Mid-Atlantic Canyons-Bathymetric, JAN 04 Detailed bathymetric coverage of offshore canyons from Gilbert Canyon to Richardson Hills including Hudson, Hatteras, Baltimore, and Wilmington Canyons. Also includes general coverage of the East Coast from Long Branch, NJ to Hilton Head Island, SC including Delaware and Chesapeake Bays. This chart does not contain the necessary detail for navigation. This is a fishing chart and should be used as a supplement to detailed BlueChart cartography. AMR-US301-4
